Welcome to The Cook Islands

Why You Should Visit

The Cook Islands are comprised of 15 individual islands near the Northeast of New Zealand between French Polynesia and American Samoa. The natives there, also known as the Maori from a Polynesian descent, are friendly and helpful people who stick to their own cultural ways. While on the Cook Islands, which ever one you want to visit, you can immerse into their way of life with multiple activities. Some activities include a Half-Day Muri Lagoon Cruise with lunch made by natives or even just relaxing on One Foot Island. Whatever activities you do will result in the best experience of your lifetime.
